## 3. "Pin Points" & The Road to "Elite" Status
To make this system truly **Hedge Fund Grade**, here is the precision roadmap.

### A. Contextual Benchmarking (The Missing Piece)
*The system currently operates in a vacuum.*
*   **Improvement**: Create a **"Competitor Agent"**.
*   **Logic**: When analyzing Couchbase, automatically scrape **MongoDB** and **Redis**.
*   **New Signal**: "Couchbase hiring slowed 10%, BUT MongoDB slowed 50%. Verdict: Couchbase is actually **Outperforming** the sector."

### B. "Customer Voice" Integration
*The system listens to the Company and the Media, but not the Users.*
*   **Improvement**: Add a **"Product Agent"**.
*   **Source**: Scrape G2, Capterra, or StackOverflow trends.
*   **Logic**: "CFO says Growth, CEO says AI, but **StackOverflow questions for 'Couchbase' dropped 15%**. Verdict: Churn Risk."

### C. The "Glass Door" (Cultural Risk)
*   **Improvement**: Analyze employee tenure.
*   **Logic**: If the CTO sees top committers from 2023 stopping activity in 2024, correlate that with LinkedIn departures.
*   **Signal**: "Brain Drain Alert: Top 3 Engineers left within 60 days."
